Overview

Purpose

The purpose of creating an online presence for my rafting business is to increase brand awareness, attract new customers, and provide a platform for customers to easily book and pay for our services online. By having an online presence, we can reach a wider audience and make it easier for customers to find and engage with our business.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website is adventure seekers who are looking for an exciting and memorable outdoor experience. They are typically between the ages of 18-45, physically fit, and enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, and water sports. They are also likely to be tech-savvy and comfortable with making online bookings and payments.


Questions: 1. Q: What types of rafting trips do you offer? A: We offer a variety of rafting trips, including half-day, full-day, and multi-day trips. Our trips range from beginner to advanced levels, so there is something for everyone.

2. Q: What safety measures do you have in place? A: Safety is our top priority, and we have trained guides who are certified in first aid and CPR. We provide all necessary safety equipment, including helmets and life jackets, and conduct safety briefings before each trip.

3. Q: Can I bring my own equipment? A: We provide all necessary equipment for our rafting trips, but if you have your own equipment that meets our safety standards, you are welcome to bring it. Please let us know in advance so we can ensure it is appropriate for the trip.
